The Excel Spreadsheet

This webpage presents the data and analysis created using Microsoft Excel as part of my CAT PAT. The spreadsheet includes:

  • Compile and clean raw survey data.
  • Calculate averages and identify trends.
  • Create graphs representing each table and users reponses.
  • Compile other raw data into readable table formats.

Excel played a crucial rolse in interpreting how people use their wearable devices.

You can access the spreadsheet here

To heres a quick guide on each of the spreadsheets:

There are a total of 2 sheets within the Excel document. This includes:

  • Costs: a table showing the min, max, and average prices for each brand and category.
  • Best Wearable: a table showing the low-end, mid-range, and high-end devices from the popular brands.
  • Survey Results: a table showing all the survey results then translated into numbers (1-5) to determine the average response. It comes with a key for what numbers mean what.
  • Survey Graphs: a spreadsheet displaying different pie charts for each question to visually represent the data.
