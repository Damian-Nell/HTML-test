The Survey Questions:

Please answer the following questions based on your personal experience with smart wearable technology (e.g., smartwatches, fitness trackers, etc.).

Q1-Q10 Rate 1-5. 1 for strongly disagree, 5 for strongly agree.

Q11 Yes/No.

Q12 Written response.

Q13 Rate 1-5. 1 for a few times a day, 5 for less than a once a week.

Q14 Rate 1-5. 1 for very unreliable, 5 for very reliable.

  1. I use a smart wearable device regularly to track my health.
  2. My smart wearable helps me stay more physically active.
  3. I am more aware of my sleep patterns because of my device.
  4. I have improved my fitness or activity levels due to the feedback from my wearable.
  5. I feel more motivated to maintain healthy habits because of my wearable.
  6. My device has helped me set and achieve health-related goals (e.g., step goals, heart rate, sleep, etc.).
  7. I check my health statistics (e.g., heart rate, steps, sleep) more often because of my wearable.
  8. The data from my wearable has influenced lifestyle changes (e.g., more exercise, better sleep).
  9. I believe my smart wearable helps prevent health issues by keeping me informed.
  10. Overall, I believe my smart wearable has positively impacted my health and well-being.
  11. My privacy and how my data is collected an influencing factor in my choice of smart wearable technology?
  12. Why? (for question 11)
  13. How often do I need to charge my wearable devices?
  14. How reliable would I say my smart wearable device is?
